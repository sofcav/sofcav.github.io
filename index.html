🛰 Space Structures · Research Internship

CubeSat Structural Design — Kyutech

Kyushu Institute of Technology · Kitakyushu, Japan · Oct 2025 – Jan 2026

Sole responsible for the complete structural subsystem of a CubeSat, from concept through Mission Definition Review (MDR). Followed JAXA and NASA mechanical standards throughout. Delivered full CAD package, technical drawings, BOMs, and stress & vibration analysis for formal international design review.

Structural Analysis
Safety factor >8 — frame passes launch load requirements per JAXA standards.
Vibration Analysis
Modal analysis — first natural frequency: 723 Hz, well above launch environment threshold.
EnduroSat Panel Fixture
Structure to hold EnduroSat panels for testing — SolidWorks design with tolerance analysis.
Biopayload Chamber
Sealed growth chamber designed to sustain a plant through the full mission — thermal control, humidity, and lighting subsystems.
Subsystem Integration
Section view showing component layout: water reservoir, ventilation, PCB, and sensor mounting.

🔩 Industrial Design · Industry Project

Welding Torch Holder — UR5e Cobot

Adicom · Tecnológico de Monterrey · Feb – Jun 2026

End-to-end design of a gripper/holder to mount an industrial welding torch on a Universal Robots UR5e cobot arm. Designed the full mechanical assembly in SolidWorks, ran stress and thermal simulation to validate performance under welding conditions, and delivered production-ready drawings and BOMs for industrial deployment.

Thermal Stress Analysis
Heat distribution along torch under welding load — max stress at torch interface, base structure remains in safe zone.
Static Safety Factor
Minimum safety factor 102 — structure exceeds mechanical requirements for robotic arm mounting.
Physical Assembly
Complete welding station with UR5e cobot, pneumatic fixture, and torch holder deployed and tested at Adicom facility.
